My background spans nonprofit grants management, international policy and development, human rights, localization, facilitation, clinical research administration, global health ethics, and community-centered program design.

My approach

I combine structured consulting tools with a human, contextual, and power-aware lens.

Grounded in context

Strategy cannot be copied and pasted. Good work starts with listening closely to the people, systems, incentives, and constraints shaping the environment.

Practical, not performative

I focus on tools organizations can actually use: clearer frameworks, sharper narratives, better questions, realistic action steps, and stronger internal alignment.

Power-aware

Localization, equity, and participation require examining who defines the problem, who holds resources, who is trusted, and who gets to decide.

Learning-oriented

I help teams turn experience into insight, insight into strategy, and strategy into grounded action.
